Our Story-

We are in textile processing since 1983.

We are vertically integrated from the fabric stage, i.e. we have

in-house processing plant, stitching plant and packing material

manufacturing unit.

We can print up to 300,000 meters per day in smaller Our width and

50,000 meter per day in wider width.

Dyeing capacity is upto 45000 meter per day in wider width

and 50000 meter in smaller width.

We can stitch and pack 150,000 sheet sets per month.

We are a one-stop destination where you can avail huge

assortment of Home Textile such as sheets, duvets, comforters,

pillow cover and curtains and printed/dyed fabric.

We export Home furnishing products to USA & Europe.

We have an innovative team that continually develops new

designs and new processes.
